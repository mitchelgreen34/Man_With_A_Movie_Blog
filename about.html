About Me


My name is Mitchel Green. I am currently working as a Cloud Solutions Engineer at Humana. I am from (and currently live in) Louisville, Kentucky. I graduated from the University of Michigan in 2023 with degrees in Data Science and Film, Television, and Media.

I've been writing about film for The Michigan Daily since March of 2021, and I didn't want to stop after I graduated from college, so I have created this website as a place for me to continue to write regularly and hopefully try some more creative pieces now that I will have more time to focus on writing.

This website will also allow me to work on my web design skills, so I will regularly be updating the look of the website, adding features to various pages, and much more.

If you'd like to reach out with feedback or comments on the website or my writing, feel free to contact me at mitchelgreen34@gmail.com.
